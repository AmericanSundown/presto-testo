A number of font families are specified. The numerical weight values (100 to 900) should show the lighter weights on the lower numbers and the heavier weights on the larger numbers. If only one font weight is available, they should all display at the same weight. The absolute keywords 'normal' and bold' are tested by the first two lines on the right hand side of the test, the third line of text tests the to 'bolder' relative keyword and the fourth tests the 'lighter' relative keyword.
